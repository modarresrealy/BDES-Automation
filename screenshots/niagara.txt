About Niagara College

Established in 1967, Niagara College (NC) is a globally recognized leader in applied education and research and is one of Canada's most enterprising post-secondary institutions.

With a mission to provide outstanding applied education and training for a changing world, NC is dedicated to offering a holistic student experience, academic excellence, global reach, alumni and partner engagement, environmental sustainability, and organizational excellence.

NC offers students hands-on, real-world learning experiences and opportunities to gain global competencies through its signature Be World Ready program, all of which support student success in the classroom and beyond.
