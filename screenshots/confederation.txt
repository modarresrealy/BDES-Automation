About Confederation College

At Confederation College, we are committed to providing our students with the skills, knowledge, and experience they need to succeed in their chosen careers. We are more than just another post-secondary institution; we're a culturally diverse, close-knit community of individuals, delivering exceptional education and training to an average of 4,853 combined full and part-time students per year.

We aim to transform lives and communities by providing high-quality education and training that meets the evolving needs of our students and the region we serve. We believe that education is the key to unlocking opportunities and creating a better future.

Featured Programs
Pharmacy Technician

Launch your career in the dynamic world of pharmacy with Confederation College's two-year Pharmacy Technician program. As a Pharmacy Technician, you'll play a crucial role in ensuring patients receive the right medications and support. This comprehensive program equips you with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in various pharmacy settings, from hospitals to community pharmacies.

Film Production

Confederation College's two-year diploma program in film production offers a comprehensive film school that covers virtually every aspect of filmmaking. From directing and screenwriting to cinematography and editing, our program provides a solid foundation in film theory and practical skills. Conflix is a leader in North America for training the next generation of film graduates.

Motive Power Techniques - Automotive

This comprehensive program covers all aspects of vehicle maintenance and repair, including electrical systems, electronics, fuels, brakes, gear trains, steering, and suspension. With hands-on instruction and practical workplace skills, you'll be well-prepared to excel in the automotive industry. Whether you're interested in working on cars or light trucks, this program provides the technical expertise and mechanical knowledge needed to succeed in today's modern and competitive job market.

Business - Accounting

Do you have strong people skills and a love of logic and reasoning? The Business - Accounting program at Confederation College is designed for students eager to develop the skills and knowledge necessary for an entry-level accounting position. This two-year program begins with a common Business first year, earning you a certificate in Business Fundamentals upon successful completion.

Featured Staff
President Michelle Salo

Michelle Salo is an accomplished leader and proud alumnus of Confederation College. She brings over 30 years of diverse leadership experience to her role as President. A certified Professional Accountant (CGA, CPA), her career spans multiple industries, including manufacturing, healthcare, and post-secondary education, showcasing her versatility.

Michelle previously served as the college's Vice President of Finance and Administration and then Chief Operating Officer. In these roles, she developed and executed strategic initiatives, managed extensive operational responsibilities, and fostered a culture of continuous improvement.

As President, Michelle is committed to fostering a culture of excellence at Confederation College, leveraging her leadership and community engagement to drive innovation and success.
