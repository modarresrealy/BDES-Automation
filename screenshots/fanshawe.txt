About Fanshawe College
The Fanshawe Advantage

Fanshawe College delivers experiential, hands-on learning opportunities to provide you with the real-world skills employers need, all while fostering a diverse, fun, and vibrant student community so you can thrive. Fanshawe is here to help you meet your goals, whether it's getting hands-on experience working with our industry partners, earning an Ontario College Diploma part-time online, or creating lifelong friendships.

Fanshawe is one of the largest colleges in Ontario, with over 200+ certificates, diplomas, advanced diplomas, and degree programs. Our London campuses and four regional campuses across Southwestern Ontario offer innovative facilities and programs that uniquely meet Ontario's labour market needs. Our programs span 17 disciplines, including tech, trades, business, and health and our faculty members have real-life experience in these fields.

Within six months of graduating, 89.7% of Fanshawe graduates are employed.

Featured Staff
Mansour Alqarni

Mansour Alqarni, Program Coordinator for Fanshawe's advanced diploma in cyber security, has been with the college for eight years. His industry experience spans threat analysis, system protection, and security research. At Fanshawe, Mansour launched "Falcon-Range," a hands-on cybersecurity training platform designed to give students hands-on training and test their skills in realistic cyber-attack simulations, positioning Fanshawe as a leader in cybersecurity education. Currently, he's developing AI solutions to enhance digital safety and is dedicated to preparing students for successful cybersecurity careers.

Residence and Housing

First-year students are guaranteed a space in residence. Fanshawe offers three traditional residences with 1,200 traditional apartment-style rooms and almost 400 townhouse rooms at our London campus. The bedrooms are private with oversized double beds. You'll have everything you need, including on-site management and security, light housekeeping, utilities, laundry, fun social activities, and more to keep you safe, secure, and comfortable.

If you'd like to live off-campus, we partner with Places4Students to help you find your new home and our housing mediation services are there to assist you if you run into any issues.

Featured Facilities: Innovation Village

With a focus on sustainability, hands-on learning, and creative problem-solving, Innovation Village is a symbol of Fanshawe's commitment to innovation in education and collaboration among students, employees, and industry partners.

Key Spaces Include:
The Diane Blake Centre of Excellence in XR, a lab focused on integrating extended reality (XR) in education
The Canada Life Village Square, a presentation and event space
The Library Learning Commons, including the Kalihwíyo Circle, a space for Indigenous students and communities to engage in research, innovation, experiential, and digital learning and social entrepreneurship
Forwell Hall, a concert, events and conference space for the Fanshawe Student Union

We’re grateful for the funding and generous donations that support this exciting new facility.
