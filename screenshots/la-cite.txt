About La Cité

Come and experience La Cité!

La Cité offers 140 programs that prepare you for the job market, authentic and highly practical learning in state-of-the-art facilities, and bachelor's degree programs that are recognized as equivalent to a university degree. But it's much more than that!

La Cité is a big family where you'll find success support services, on-campus health services, competitive sports teams, and a host of other benefits.

As the largest French-language college of applied arts and technology in Ontario, we have trained some 40,000 proud graduates who are now making their mark in Ontario, Canada, and around the world. Join them today!
