About Collège Boréal

Attending Collège Boréal means choosing to study and immerse yourself in a French-speaking environment while being trained to work in a bilingual job market. At Collège Boréal, your professors teach you the terms of the industry in both French and English. Why? Because our goal is for you to succeed, and being bilingual is a huge asset in the marketplace.

Explore the Boréal Guarantee: There are no guarantees in life … except at Collège Boréal!
