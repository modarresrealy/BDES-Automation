About St. Lawrence College

St. Lawrence College (SLC) offers programs and modern, expansive facilities on three southeastern Ontario campuses in Kingston, Brockville, and Cornwall. Here, you'll get to know your professors, who are professionals with industry experience, and gain the knowledge and training to be career-ready with state-of-the-art learning facilities and a close-knit college environment.

Featured Programs
Bachelor of Commerce: Social Innovation

The Bachelor of Commerce: Social Innovation program is a unique, three-year degree designed for individuals passionate about enhancing social and sectoral outcomes through applied innovation, systems thinking, organizational resilience, and entrepreneurial solutions. This program equips students with the tools to tackle a range of business and societal challenges like climate change, aging populations, and urbanization.
