About Seneca Polytechnic

Seneca Polytechnic provides a great education to prepare you for a great career. Combining academic rigour with practical, professional, and career-focused learning, we offer a seamless transition from education to employment. Expert faculty, excellent staff, outstanding campuses, awesome technology, deep connections with industry: This is Seneca Polytechnic. 

From business, health, and technology to creative arts, engineering, and more, Seneca has the programs and learning options to fuel your ambition. You’ll get a bespoke education that blends theory and practice, combining the best of university and college. You’ll work on real projects in purpose-built labs and acquire career-focused skills and valuable connections. You’ll also benefit from industry exposure that prepares you with the skills to graduate career ready and world ready.

And that’s not all. Our flexible learning options are designed to fit your schedule and goals. As a leader in academic pathways, we offer a range of pathway opportunities to help you achieve success. If you have prior post-secondary experience, you can choose to transfer to a program at Seneca, between programs at Seneca, or to other institutions beyond Seneca. You’re a learner and a leader and, at Seneca, we’re ready to meet you where you are.

The Service Hub

Have a general question or need help with a more complex issue? At Seneca, you can simply contact The Service Hub - your answer is just a click away. The Service Hub is a centralized source of information on everything from registration to athletics, libraries, counselling, and much more. Available across nine channels, it's the first-of-its-kind approach to student support and services.

Health & Wellness Centre

Taking care of your physical, mental, and spiritual wellness helps you excel at what you do. That's why we're making space for a new Health & Wellness Centre at our Newnham Campus. Building on the great wellness activities Seneca already offers, this new Centre will be a transformative destination for health services, teaching and learning, athletics, recreation, and spiritual connection.
