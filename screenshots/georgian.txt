About Georgian College

Experience MORE at Georgian College. As a recognized leader in hands-on, career-focused education, Georgian takes you beyond the classroom and into your industry, helping you build the skills, confidence, and connections to succeed.

As a leader in student work experience, we’ll help you stand out with:

130+ career-focused programs – degrees, diplomas, and certificates.
Smaller classes and personalized support.
Work-integrated learning in 100% of our programs.
Combined degree–diploma pathways for even more opportunities.
Flexible study options – full-time, part-time, online, in-person, or hybrid.
Strong employer connections across multiple industries.
State-of-the-art facilities and programs in key growth areas like health, skilled trades, and emerging technologies.
