About Durham College

Be ready for today's competitive workforce with one of Durham College's 145 programs, designed in response to industry demand to provide you with valuable skills and experience.

Start your learning in our modern and innovative labs and classrooms with our expert faculty. Then, go beyond the textbooks to gain invaluable hands-on experience in real-world settings through co-ops, field placements, work terms, or other opportunities.

Hands-on Learning at Durham College

At Durham College, we prioritize hands-on learning to enhance skills, provide networking opportunities, and gain valuable real-world work experience, all before graduation.

From simulations and integrative exercises built right into the curriculum to field placements and co-ops, many different types of experiential learning provide students with a range of experiences.
