About Humber Polytechnic

Humber is committed to equipping students with the skills they need for lifelong employability and to become the person they always knew was within them.

As a global leader in polytechnic education, Humber students receive in-depth theoretical learning and hands-on experience with applied research and extensive industry connections. Like the world around us, Humber is constantly in motion, creating a new expectation for education, while helping solve challenges faced by our world and communities, including Canada's productivity crisis.

Featured Programs
Bachelor of Engineering - Mechatronics

The Bachelor of Engineering - Mechatronics discipline gives students hands-on experience with industry-standard advanced technology. With a focus on robotics or embedded systems, students will solve real industry and community problems and gain knowledge and skills in mechanical, electrical, electronics and computer engineering.

Bachelor of Science - Nursing

Humber was the first college in Ontario to launch its independent nursing degree in 2021. Humber's leadership in nursing education continues with the establishment of the Humber-Osler Academic Practice Partnership, the first formal collaboration of its kind in Canada between a college and a health system for nursing education, scholarship, practice, and the advancement of excellence in patient care.

Apply
Forensic Identification

As the only program of its kind in Ontario, students will study advanced fingerprint development and analysis, physical matching of footwear and tool marks, DNA and blood profiling, death investigations, rules of evidence, and trial procedure. Aspiring investigators get experience in a crime scene studio and Humber's forensic lab.

Comedy Writing and Performance

In this diploma program, students learn the craft of stand-up, improvisation, scriptwriting, and sketch comedy. The instructors are award-winning professionals who are active in the comedy industry.
